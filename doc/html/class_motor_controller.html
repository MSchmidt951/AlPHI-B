void MotorController::addChange ( float  PIDchange[3][3],
int  axis,
int  pA,
int  pB,
int  nA,
int  nB 
)

Use the PID controls to add a change per motor on a certain axis.

The axis is split in two (positive and negative). The positive side adds the change and the negative side subtracts the change.

Parameters
[in]PIDchangeSee PIDcontroller::PIDchange
[in]axisWhich axis to add change to (0: roll, 1: pitch, 2:yaw)
[in]pAIndex of motor A of the positive side
[in]pBIndex of motor B of the positive side
[in]nAIndex of motor A of the negative side
[in]nBIndex of motor B of the negative side
